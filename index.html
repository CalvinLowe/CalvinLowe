Calvin Lowe is freelance a web developer living in Brisbane, Australia. Professionally he specialises in helping small business create and optimise their prescence on the web.

When Calvin is not helping small businesses implement their ideas on the web, he is busy studying in the Master of Information Technology program at The University of Queensland.

Calvin also has a Bachelor of Science with a major in Biophysics and in his previous life worked as a Quality Control Chemist. His love and of knowledge of science can occasionally be seen in some previous writing. Calvin reserves the right to blog on any topic between science and technology and the web.

If you made it this far and would like to work with me. I'd love to hear from you.
